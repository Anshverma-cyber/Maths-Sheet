Our Mission

Mera naam Ansh Verma hai. Maine **Coding Unlocked** ko isliye banaya hai taki main students ki help kar sakun. Hamara maqsad complex cheezon ko simple banana hai, chahe woh coding ho ya mathematics.

About Maths-Sheet

**Maths-Sheet** hamara ek khaas project hai. Is website par humne Tables (1-50), Squares (1-30), aur Cubes (1-30) jaise important tools ko ek jagah jama kiya hai. Yeh platform un sabhi ke liye hai jo apni calculations fast banana chahte hain aur exams ki taiyari kar rahe hain.

Who Am I?

Main ek developer hoon jo naye tools aur websites banane ka shauk rakhta hoon. Coding ke saath-saath mujhe digital awareness aur education mein interest hai. Main hamesha koshish karta hoon ki mere projects users ke liye helpful aur easy-to-use hon.
